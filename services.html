About Us

In 2001 Bob saw the need for professional pool care in the Ilam area and set up a small business with a few select clients. From these humble beginnings, and because of Bobs exceptional customer service, Bobs Pool Service has now grown to include another full-time pool service technician and can cover the greater Ilam area and beyond with a wide range of services:

  • Pool cleaning
  • Filter cleaning / backflushing / replacement
  • Check / maintain PH levels
  • General pool advice
  • Retrofitting and repairs for pools and spas
  • Cleaning for equine training pools
